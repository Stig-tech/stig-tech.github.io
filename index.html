Vulcan, as seen above, is the largest and heaviest cast iron statue in the world, standing 56 feet tall and weighing in at over 100,000 pounds. Made entirely of cast iron, designed by Italian sculptor Giuseppe Moretti, the statue was created for the 1904 Louisiana Purchase Exposition in St. Louis, Missouri, to symbolize Birmingham's iron and steel industry.
